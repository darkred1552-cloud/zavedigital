SEO Google Guide

Social Media Marketing — Restaurants Ke Liye Golden Rules

Facebook aur Instagram pe restaurant kaise promote karein? Daily posting, reels, engagement — sab kuch step by step...

Social Media Restaurant Facebook

Daily Routine:

  • Morning: 1 food photo/story post karo (breakfast items)
  • Afternoon: Special offer ya deal share karo
  • Evening: Reel banao (cooking video, customer review)
  • Night: DMs aur replies karo

Free Growth Tips:

  • Hashtags: #LahoreFood #PizzaLahore #FoodiePakistan (location-based use karo)
  • Posting Time: 12-2 PM ya 7-9 PM (zyada log active hote hain)
  • Reels 3x zyada reach karte hain vs photos
  • Comments ka reply karo — algorithm pasand karta hai

Pro Tip: Weekly 1 viral-worthy reel banao — food plating, behind scenes, customer reactions.

WhatsApp Business Setup — Complete Guide 2026

WhatsApp Business account kese banayein? Catalog, auto-replies, quick replies — sab free mein setup karo...

WhatsApp Business Free

Setup Steps:

  1. Phone pe WhatsApp Business app download karo (free)
  2. Business profile setup: name, address, hours, category
  3. Catalog add karo: products/services with photos + prices
  4. Greeting message set karo (auto-reply for new customers)
  5. Away message set karo (after hours auto-reply)
  6. Quick replies bao (shortcuts for common questions)
  7. Labels lagao (new customer, paid, pending, etc.)

WhatsApp vs Regular — Difference:

  • Business badge ✅ (green tick ke baad)
  • Catalog feature ✅
  • Auto-replies ✅
  • Business hours ✅
  • Web version ✅ (web.whatsapp.com)

Pro Tip: Catalog mein har product ka photo acha hona chahiye — white background, clear image, sahi price.

Website Banwani Hai? — Complete Cost & Process Guide

Pakistan mein website kitni cost mein banti hai? Kya kya options hain? Kaunsa choose karein — sab ka answer...

Website Cost Guide

Website Types & Pricing (Pakistan):

  • Static Website (1-5 pages): Rs. 5,000 - 15,000
  • Business Website (5-10 pages): Rs. 12,000 - 30,000
  • E-Commerce Store: Rs. 25,000 - 80,000
  • Custom Web App: Rs. 50,000 - 2,00,000+

Free Options (Zero Budget):

  • Google Sites: Free, simple pages
  • GitHub Pages: Free, developer needed
  • WordPress.com: Free with .wordpress.com domain
  • Wix/Weebly: Free with ads

Kya Chahiye Professional Website Mein:

  • ✅ Custom domain (.com / .pk)
  • ✅ Mobile responsive design
  • ✅ SEO optimized
  • ✅ Contact form + WhatsApp
  • ✅ Fast loading (under 3 seconds)

Facebook Ads — Chhote Budget Se Shuru Karna Hai

Sirf Rs. 500/day se Facebook ads kaise chalayein? Targeting, creative, budget — complete beginner guide in Roman Urdu...

Facebook Ads Budget Marketing

Rs. 500/Day Strategy:

  • Audience: City-wide, age 25-45, interests (food, shopping, business)
  • Ad Type: Single image ya short video (15-30 sec)
  • Goal: Messages ya Website Clicks (choose based on need)
  • Duration: 7 days minimum (koi bhi result 1-2 din mein nahi aata)

Creative Tips:

  • Before/After photos (transformation dikhao)
  • Customer reviews/videos best performers hain
  • Offer clear rakho ("50% OFF this week only")
  • Urdu/Roman Urdu mein text — English se zyada engagement milti hai locally

Mistakes to Avoid:

  • ❌ Nationwide targeting (budget waste hoga)
  • ❌ Single din mein mat rukna — 3-7 din to chahiye
  • ❌ Poor quality photos (blurry, dark)
  • ❌ No call to action ("Order Now", "Message Us")
